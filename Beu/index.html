Frequently Asked Questions

Q1

What age groups do you accept?

We welcome students from 3 years old to adults.

Q2

Do I need dance experience to join?

No experience needed! We cater to all levels including beginners, intermediate, and advanced dancers. Our Adult classes are specifically designed to be stress-free and beginner-friendly.

Q3

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable clothes (leggings, joggers, or athletic wear) and covered sports shoes. Don't forget to bring a water bottle!

Q4

Can I switch styles using my package?

Yes! Our Flexi Packages (Credit Packages) allow you to switch between different dance styles so you can explore everything we offer.

Q5

Do you prepare students for competitions?

Yes! We provide dedicated Performance Training, Stage Exposure, and Competition Coaching for students looking to take their dancing to the next level.

Q6

Are trial classes available?

Yes! trial classes are available based on our current schedule. Please contact us to find a slot that fits you!
